Easter Egg Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Easter eggs below. By clicking on an Easter egg, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You will lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Easter egg is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each Easter egg.
